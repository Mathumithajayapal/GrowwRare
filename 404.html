Year

First Edition

About this Collection

A landmark in English literature, this first edition of Lyrical Ballads represents the beginning of the Romantic movement. Published anonymously in 1798, it contains Coleridge's "The Rime of the Ancient Mariner" and Wordsworth's "Tintern Abbey" among other revolutionary poems.

This copy is exceptionally well-preserved, with original boards and uncut pages. It includes the famous Advertisement that served as a manifesto for the new poetry.

Features

  • Original boards with paper spine label
  • Complete with half-title and advertisement leaf
  • Uncut and untrimmed pages
  • Contemporary ownership inscription
  • Expertly rebacked with original spine preserved
  • Includes errata leaf
