Authentication

Verification and certification

How do I purchase a rare book?

You can purchase directly from our catalog or participate in our exclusive auctions. Create an account to get started, and our team will guide you through the process.

How do you authenticate rare books?

Our experts examine provenance, binding, paper quality, typography, and other historical markers. Each book comes with a certificate of authenticity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, credit cards (for purchases under $10,000), and cryptocurrency for qualified buyers. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Do you offer restoration services?

Yes, we have a team of expert conservators who specialize in rare book restoration. Contact us for a consultation and quote.

Can I place items on hold?

Items can be held for 48 hours with a 10% deposit. For our VIP members, we offer extended holding periods.

What is your return policy?

All sales are final, but we guarantee authenticity. If a book is found to be inauthentic, we offer a full refund within 30 days.

Do you offer shipping insurance?

Yes, all shipments are fully insured and tracked. International shipping includes customs documentation and special handling.

How do I become a VIP member?

VIP membership is by invitation or after reaching $50,000 in purchases. Benefits include first access to new acquisitions and private viewings.
