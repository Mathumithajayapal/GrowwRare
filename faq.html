How do I purchase a rare book?

You can purchase directly from our catalog or participate in our exclusive auctions. Create an account to get started, and our team will guide you through the process. We offer secure checkout and worldwide shipping.

How do you authenticate rare books?

Our experts examine provenance, binding, paper quality, typography, and other historical markers. Each book comes with a certificate of authenticity signed by our lead curator.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, credit cards (for purchases under $10,000), and cryptocurrency for qualified buyers. All payments are secure and encrypted with bank-level security.

Do you offer restoration services?

Yes, we have a team of expert conservators who specialize in rare book restoration. Contact us for a consultation and quote. Services include leather binding repair, paper restoration, and custom slipcases.

Can I place items on hold?

Items can be held for 48 hours with a 10% deposit. For our VIP members, we offer extended holding periods up to 30 days.

What is your return policy?

All sales are final, but we guarantee authenticity. If a book is found to be inauthentic by a certified expert, we offer a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

Do you offer shipping insurance?

Yes, all shipments are fully insured and tracked. International shipping includes customs documentation and special handling for valuable items.

How long does international shipping take?

International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Express shipping options are available for urgent deliveries.

How do I become a VIP member?

VIP membership is by invitation or after reaching $50,000 in purchases. Benefits include first access to new acquisitions, private viewings, and exclusive auction previews.

Can I sell my books through Vintage Volumes?

Yes, we offer consignment services for rare books and manuscripts. Contact our acquisitions team for a free evaluation and consultation.
